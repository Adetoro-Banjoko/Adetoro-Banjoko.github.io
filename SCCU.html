Superior Choice Credit Union

"Provide superior service while delivering products and services that meet the financial needs of our members" is the driving mission behind Superior Choice Credit Union. They identified that their current site was begging to fall short of this mission statement and didn't represent their unique vibe and presence in the credit union space. They wanted something that dint feel like a “blocky template” and sought a glimpse of what the future of their customer experience could be with a custom design and build.

Loading up my trusty Figma canvas I got to work on refreshing the homepage. My mindset was to elevate not eradicate, levying what aspects of the brand worked and picking up the slack on aspects that were lackluster. In the end I delivered a design that breaks out of the templated structure and expediates the user flow by immediately funneling different user types to their respective needs.
